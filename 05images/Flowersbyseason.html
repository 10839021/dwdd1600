Planning Ahead

This section will give you the tools to decide when to schedule your photoshoot. All of the following locations are located in Utah. Please reach out with your intened package and location so we can finalize an invoice for you.

Moab Utah

Beautiful red rock will be the perfect pop of color for you and your family photos, engagement photoshoot, or even pet session! Don't underestimate this beautiful location. Be sure to bring the following items so your adventure is one full of fun...

  • Hiking shoes for the trail
  • Shoes for the photoshoot
  • Lots of water and a packed lunch
  • Any additional outfits and a backpack

Utah Valley Forest

This scenic prefference includes multiple locations. Please note that the enterance fee for some of the state, and National parks, isn't included in the final invoice.

Utah Lake

Various marinas and docks around Utah lake provide a heaven like scene perfect for any photoshoot. However, with water comes the risk of having to re-schedule. If you'd like to add an additional $50 insurance, if anyone falls in you can re-book your session completely free.
