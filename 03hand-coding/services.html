Family Portraits

This service is priced by the number of family members. The following prices also reflect if individual, and couples pictures will take place. Please review the factors below for a relative quote. Please note that the season, time, place, and distance to travel may affect your official quote.

  • Couples session - 2 Adults - 1.5 hours: $179
  • Small Family Session - 2 Adults, 1-2 Kids - 2 hours: $257
  • Growing Family Session - 2-3 Adults, 3-5 Kids - 2.5 hours: $307
  • Large Family Session - 2-8 Adults, 4-10 kids - 3 hours: $550
  • Extended Family Session - 15+ Adults, 30+ Kids - 4 Hours: $1237

If additional time is requested, and available, each additional 30min is $35. Your paid starts at the agreed-upon time, even if the whole group hasn't arrived. Please reach out as soon as possible if there are extenuating circumstances or accidents beyond your control and we will do our best to be accommodating as a courtesy.

What Constitutes Extenuating Circumstances?

Sudden Illness or Injury
If you, or anyone in your immediate family, needs to be hospitalized suddenly. This does not include minor cold symtoms. However if you are unable to travel in your condition, this constitues a free of charge re-schedule.
Car troubles or Motorvechical Accident
If your car will not start and you have no other resources to make the photoshoot, it can be re-scheduled free of charge.
If you or, anyone in the party is involved in an accident of this sort and can't make the scheduled time the session can be rescheduled free of charge
Facial Injuries
If a member of the party scheduled for the photoshoot receives a facial injury, or any injury that would appear in the photos, you have the right to reschedule at any time. However you must let the Rowan Rose team know at least 24 hours before if possible for the re-schedule fee to be avoided, $50.
